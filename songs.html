Over There! is an example of a classic popular World War I song. It was more often sung by civilians and untried recruits who had not seen the front line yet. The grim humor and raw emotions of trenches created a more realistic and darker breed of music known as “Trench Songs”, where men took the jaunty, uplifting popular tunes, and set them to dark, graphic lyrics. Below are lyrics and recordings of some of these songs.
